# Zanta AI

[Zanta AI](https://zanta.ai/) is an all-in-one AI image and video studio for creators, marketers, founders, ecommerce teams, and product builders. It brings image generation, video generation, visual editing, asset cleanup, and creative testing into one browser-based workflow.

Use Zanta AI when you need to move from an idea, prompt, reference image, or product concept into usable creative assets for landing pages, ads, thumbnails, product visuals, launch materials, and short social videos.

## AI Image and Video Workflows

Zanta AI supports the core creative workflows that small teams usually need when shipping visual content quickly:

- Create product shots, ad concepts, thumbnails, and landing page visuals with the [AI image generator](https://zanta.ai/ai-image).
- Turn text prompts or source images into short clips with the [AI video generator](https://zanta.ai/ai-video).
- Explore fast image generation through the [Nano Banana AI image generator](https://zanta.ai/image/nano-banana).
- Create and edit visuals with the [GPT Image 2 AI image generator](https://zanta.ai/image/gpt-image-2).
- Clean up production assets with a [background remover](https://zanta.ai/ai-tools/background-remover), improve resolution with an [image upscaler](https://zanta.ai/ai-tools/image-upscaler), and convert reference images into reusable prompts with an [image to prompt tool](https://zanta.ai/ai-tools/image-to-prompt).
