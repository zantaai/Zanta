# Zanta AI

[Zanta AI](https://zanta.ai) is an all-in-one AI image and video studio for creators, marketers, founders, and product teams. It helps you generate, edit, and compare creative assets from prompts, source images, and product ideas in one browser-based workflow.

Use Zanta AI as an [AI image and video studio](https://zanta.ai) for product visuals, ad concepts, thumbnails, short social clips, launch assets, and creative testing.

## Core AI Creative Workflows

- Create visuals with an [AI image generator](https://zanta.ai/ai-image) for product shots, ads, thumbnails, and landing page assets.
- Turn prompts or source images into motion with an [AI video generator](https://zanta.ai/ai-video) for social clips, demos, and campaign concepts.
- Explore a [Nano Banana AI image generator](https://zanta.ai/image/nano-banana) workflow for fast creative image generation.
- Use a [GPT Image 2 AI image generator](https://zanta.ai/image/gpt-image-2) workflow for image creation and visual editing.
- Clean up images with a [background remover](https://zanta.ai/ai-tools/background-remover), improve assets with an [image upscaler](https://zanta.ai/ai-tools/image-upscaler), and turn references into reusable prompts with an [image to prompt tool](https://zanta.ai/ai-tools/image-to-prompt).

## Who Zanta AI Is For

Zanta AI is designed for people who need to move quickly from idea to visual output:

- Solo founders building landing pages, ads, and launch assets.
- Marketers testing campaign concepts and social visuals.
- Ecommerce teams creating product images and promotional creatives.
- Content creators producing thumbnails, short videos, and visual stories.
- Designers and builders exploring AI-assisted creative directions.
